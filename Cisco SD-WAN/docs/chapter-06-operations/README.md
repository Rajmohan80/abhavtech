# Operations & Monitoring

This chapter covers comprehensive operational procedures for managing Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN including monitoring, alerting, analytics, troubleshooting, backup/restore, upgrades, change management, and incident response.

## Chapter Overview

Effective operations ensure SD-WAN delivers consistent performance and reliability. This chapter provides operational frameworks for:

- vManage dashboards and monitoring
- Alerting configuration and analytics
- Troubleshooting methodologies
- Software upgrades and configuration management
- Change and incident management
- Performance optimization and capacity planning
- NOC operations and compliance auditing
