# Chapter 8 — Case Study

A complete end-to-end worked example applying every formula and decision framework from Chapters 1–7 to a real enterprise scenario.

## In this chapter

- [Enterprise CC Example](enterprise-cc-example.md) — Bharatiya Fintech Bank: 1,200-agent AI contact centre across four Indian cities

## Why read the case study

The case study shows how individual variables interact. In isolation, each formula is straightforward. Together, they reveal non-obvious design decisions — for example, why upgrading bandwidth alone from 10G to 40G still leaves IS = 9.8 (insufficient), but combining a 10G WAN with campus-edge AI brings IS to 2.2 (optimal). The interaction effects are the point.
