# Policies & Traffic Engineering

This chapter covers comprehensive SD-WAN policy design including application-aware routing, QoS, data/control policies, direct Internet access, service insertion, and advanced traffic optimization techniques.

## Chapter Overview

SD-WAN policies enable intelligent traffic steering, QoS enforcement, and service insertion. This chapter provides detailed policy design patterns for:

- Application-aware routing based on SLA requirements
- QoS policy for voice, video, and data prioritization
- Data and control policy for traffic manipulation
- Direct Internet access and local breakout
- Service insertion and traffic chaining
- Performance optimization techniques

- **4.1 Policy Framework Overview** - Policy architecture and components

- **4.5 Control Policy Design** - Route manipulation at control plane
- **4.6 Access Control Lists** - Traffic filtering and security policies
- **4.7 Direct Internet Access** - Local Internet breakout policies
- **4.8 Service Insertion** - Firewall and service chaining

- **4.10 TCP Optimization** - WAN optimization techniques
- **4.11 Multicast Policy** - Multicast traffic handling
- **4.12 Policy Troubleshooting & Validation** - Testing and verification

## Policy Types

**Centralized Policies** (applied at vSmart):
- Application-Aware Routing
- Traffic data policies
- Control plane policies

**Localized Policies** (applied at WAN Edge):
- Access control lists
- QoS policies
- Route policies
